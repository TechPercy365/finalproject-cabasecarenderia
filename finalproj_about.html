ABOUT THEIR BUSINESS

Cabase's Carenderia started in 2004 with only P500 as their investment to build this small business. Years passed by, their carenderia grows and improved that many customers are now buy their meal for breakfast and lunch such as students, workers and people that need a quick serving for their meal. This business which is carenderia serves a great impact to the owner's life not just the people surrounded by it. It is because it change how the way she live and became her part of growing up.

BENEFITS OF THEIR BUSINESS IN THEIR LIFE

Their business serves great impact to the owner, it is because this has been their way of meeting their needs. For instance, food resources since their business is carenderia. In addition to this, It is also a way to meet their financial needs such as electric and water bills. Also, utilities and maintenance for their house.

PREPARATION

In 2:00 P.M, they're gonna go to the market to buy ingredients for their dishes to serve for tomorrow. After buying all their ingredients, they now prepared it for tomorrow such as slicing ingredients such as onions, garlic, vegetables. Also marinating the meat such as chicken and pork. Then they will wake up at 2:00 A.M to start cooking. It will be done at 6:00 P.M, exact time to the customers to start their day.

EARNINGS

They earn 5,000 to 7,000 pesos a day in their Carenderia. But due to the pandemic, it became less and it comes to the point where they stop their business due to their financial problems. But after they counter the challenges and solve their problems, their business runs again and now can earn 4,000 to 5,000 pesos.
